## 1. Purpose
This document provides the high-level business architecture for Global Tutor, outlining the strategic business drivers, target capabilities, process flows, and value realization.

## 2. Business Position & Value Proposition
- **Position**: Enterprise Learning & Knowledge Marketplace
- **Description**: Connects internal experts and learners, manages scheduling/bookings, and validates skill development.
- **Target Value**: Addresses operational bottlenecks, automates core knowledge consolidation, and accelerates reporting or execution speed to reduce decision latency and cost.

## 3. Business Capabilities
1. **Intelligent Capture & Synthesis**: Instantly aggregates raw input signals into unified semantic domains.
2. **Dynamic Decision Intelligence**: Powers predictive and structured analytical views for key enterprise stakeholders.
3. **Automated Governance Compliance**: Audits workflows and tracks compliance rules natively.

## 4. Business Flow
1. **Ingest (Problems / Context)**: Data files, operational feeds, regulatory changes, or project specifications.
2. **Process (Platform Core)**: Synthesis, relationship graphing, or agentic compiler pipelines.
3. **Output (Business Outcomes)**: Direct briefing delivery, compiled deployment code, or vendor catalog charts.
